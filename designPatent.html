Powerful Protection and Easy to Get

A design patent is infringed if an ordinary observer would think that the accused design is substantially the same as the patented design when the two designs are compared in the context of the prior art. Apple was awarded $290 million in damages on their design patent. Design patents are easier to get than “utility” patents. All patents, including design patents, are examined by the patent office before the patent is granted. For a design patent, however, the patent office rarely makes any kind of rejection of the design patent, because most designs are unique. So in many ways it is easier to get a design patent, than it is to get a utility patent. Most design patents get allowed by the patent office right away.
