Utility Patent Law:

Patent law is an area of law that assists an inventor’s exclusive right to use their own invention. Patent law aims to encourage new products and inventions by granting creators the legal right to use and profit from the things that they create. As patent attorneys, we help clients apply for patents and license patents.

Trademark Law:

Trademark law is the set of legal regulations set up to protect trademarks. A trademark is a legal protection given to any word, name, symbol, or design that is used in commerce to identify the product of one manufacturer from another. A federal trademark protects the branding of your product or service..

Product Design Patents :

Design patents are designed to protect the ornamental look of your invention. A design patent will protect you against others copying the way your invention looks. Design patents can be obtained on many different kinds of things including fabric and fabric designs, clothing, packaging of a product, computer user interfaces or icons and electronics.
